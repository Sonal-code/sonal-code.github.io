✅ Section 6 — What to Do Next

4 Actions That Move the Needle

These are the highest-leverage moves for the Williams family right now, in order of impact.

🏠
Switch Investment Properties to P&I Repayments
IPs are likely on interest-only loans. Switching to principal & interest adds ~$3,216/mo to repayments but builds equity fast and guarantees debt-free by 65. Talk to your broker about offset accounts to manage cashflow.
Impact: Clears $1.94M debt by retirement ↑↑↑
💰
Increase Monthly Savings to $4,167 (20% of income)
Currently saving $2,000/mo (9.6%). Hitting 20% means an extra $2,167/mo into savings or investments. Over 30 years, this doubles the savings pot from $1.56M to $3.14M.
Impact: +$1.58M extra at retirement ↑↑
🍔
Reduce Takeaway Spend by 50%
$253/mo on takeaway is a small leak but symbolic of discretionary habits. Cutting to $127/mo saves $1,524/year — redirect to savings or extra mortgage repayments.
Impact: $1,524/yr freed up ↑
🛡️
Build Emergency Buffer to 6 Months of Expenses
Current savings of $51k = ~2.5 months of total expenses. Target is 6 months ($120k–$130k). This protects against rate rises, vacant IPs, or job changes without touching investments.
Impact: Financial resilience ↑
