Your Financial Snapshot

👤 What a Client gets from this

  • A clear picture of exactly where every dollar goes each month
  • An honest financial health score — no jargon, just a number with context
  • The gap between what they're saving now and what they need to be saving
  • Whether their mortgage repayments will actually pay off before they retire
  • A projected net worth at age 65 — so retirement feels real, not abstract
  • 3–4 specific actions they can take right now to improve their position

🏦 What a Broker / Adviser gets from this

  • Combined LVR across all properties — debt concentration at a glance
  • Rental yield vs. mortgage cost — cashflow position per investment property
  • The repayment gap to achieve debt-free status at retirement
  • Savings rate vs. income — headroom for additional repayments or investing
  • Emergency buffer size — is the client exposed if rates rise or a tenant leaves?
  • A ready-made conversation starter — show the client their own numbers
